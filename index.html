mobile-first wallet

Designed for daily Octra use.

Poctra keeps the wallet surface compact: balances, activity, assets, trade, bridge, and browser workflows stay one tap away without turning the app into a dashboard maze.

Overview

Octra wallet, EVM account, and activity in one calm surface.

Manage multiple wallets, see Octra Identity names, review recent operations, and keep address-aware send flows close to the balance screen.

  • Multi-wallet drawer with naming and reorder support
  • Octra Identity lookup for recipients and wallet labels
  • Portfolio view and activity center for cross-account history

dApps, oct://, and Circle apps from one browser.

  • Octra Identity provider management

security-first UX

Built for sensitive keys and high-context approvals.

Poctra treats every signing moment as a review moment: backup keys require re-auth, dApp approvals use bottom sheets, and send flows show recipient resolution before execution.

Backup Keys

Reveal mnemonic, Octra Base64 keys, and derived EVM hex keys only after biometric or PIN verification.

Preflight Checks

Review address book status, ENS or Octra Identity resolution, gas readiness, and network compatibility.

Approval Sheets

Connection requests, swaps, bridge steps, and sends use focused bottom review sheets before signing.
